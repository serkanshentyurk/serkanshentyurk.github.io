About

I am Turkish/Bulgarian Optical Biology PhD student at UCL.

I have obtained BSc in Molecular Biology and Genetics with summa cum laude from Bilkent University and a MSc in Statistics and Data Science from KU Leuven as a Science@Leuven scholar. Previously, I have worked at Gonçalves Lab and Bonin Lab at NERF, a research institute empowered by imec, VIB and KU Leuven and did my thesis research under Dr. Bart De Moor's PhD candidate Melanie Nijs' supervision.

Final note, Dr. Gonçalves is currently hiring at PhD/postdoc levels: see open positions at goncalveslab.sites.vib.be.
