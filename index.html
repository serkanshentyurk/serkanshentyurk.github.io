I am Serkan (Серкан Шентюрк), a PhD student at Learning, Inference, and Memory (LIM) Lab at Sainsbury Wellcome Centre (SWC), University College London (UCL) as an Wellcome Trust - Optical Biology PhD student.

I hold a BSc in Molecular Biology and Genetics from Bilkent University, Turkey, and an MSc in Statistics and Data Science from KU Leuven, Belgium.

I was previously a member of the Gonçalves Lab at the Neuro-Electronics Research Flanders (NERF) in Belgium. I am interested in how neural circuits optimise and evolve their dynamics during learning.
