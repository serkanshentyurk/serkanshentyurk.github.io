About

I am Turkish/Bulgarian graduate researcher at Gonçalves Lab and a second-year Statistics and Data Science master student at KU Leuven as a Science@Leuven scholar. Currently, I am working with Dr. Pedro Gonçalves on the modelling of pyloric network of Cancer borealis at NERF, a research institute empowered by imec, VIB and KU Leuven and doing my thesis research on denoising of Mass Spectrophotometry Imaging (MSI) data under Dr. Bart De Moor's PhD candidate Melanie Nijs' supervision.

I studied Molecular Biology and Genetics at Bilkent University and graduated with Summa cum laude. I did my internships at different labs on the topics related to Behavioural Neuroscience and my interests have started to shift from Behavioural Neuroscience to Computational Neuroscience. I belive MSc in Statistics is going to be a sufficient preparatory step for me to do cutting-edge research during my PhD studies and afterwards (I hope :) ). You can find more about my backgroud at Resume.

Final note, Dr. Gonçalves is currently hiring at PhD/postdoc levels: see open positions at goncalveslab.sites.vib.be.
